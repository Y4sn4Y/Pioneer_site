press & block

As previously mentioned, the bhv_basic_move.cpp file contains many functions, including the "block" and "press" functions, which serve the same general purpose:
preventing the opponent from gaining full control of the ball during the game. Taking into account various factors such as distance, etc.
, the "press" function determines the best opponent to press, and the player attempts to prevent the ball from being passed to the selected opponent. The "block" function operates in a similar manner, with the key difference being that it selects the second closest opponent to the ball. This second closest opponent is likely to be the target for the player closest to the ball who is planning to pass to a teammate.
Therefore, this function can prevent the pass from occurring by positioning a player close to the intended recipient, thus excluding them from the list of potential recipients for the opponent's pass. As a result, this function decreases the likelihood of a successful pass for the opponent, giving us an advantage in gaining control of the ball.
