shoot_better

In the "Shoot_Better" section of the code, we adjust the shooting strategy based on the player's position and the presence of opponents.
By prioritizing the furthest corner from the goalie as the primary shooting location, we create more space to shoot and widen the options available.
Additionally, areas with opponents are fully closed off for shooting. Furthermore, we refine the power and speed of shooting based on the player's distance from the goalie
